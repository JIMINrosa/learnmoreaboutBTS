BTS IMPACT

The 7 member group BTS have taken KPOP to not only be more popular but also more opportunities for others to be respected. They have started getting more recognized after their first live performance on USA television in 2017 for the American Music Awards. They were the first KPOP act to do so following to their sold out stadium Love Yourself tour in 2018. Their popualrity continues to grow after them being the first KPOP act to reach #1 on Billboard Hot 100 with Dynamite. Why the popularity? BTS over the years show their passion toward music from nonstop dance practices, writing songs, and producing their music. They also express kindness and humbleness since they know where they started, which is a small bankrupt agency in Seoul. Their messages throughout their songs inspired many fans from finding yourself, losing their way, and fear of the unknown. They also have their own show called RUN BTS to get to know them more, plus having livestreams, and short videos of their music production and award ceremonies.

BTS'S JOURNEY

BTS debuted on June 13, 2013 where they first performed to the public at Mnet Stage of their songs "No More Dream" and "We are Bulletproof pt2".

On July 9, 2013 where BTS announced their fandom named called ARMY!

On September 11, 2013 BTS released their first mini album called O!RUL8,2?.

It was difficult for them to reach for spotlight as they were looked down upon from not coming from a well known agency called BigHit. The most well known companies are called "The Big Three" which is YG, SM, and JYP entertainment.

BTS kept releasing mini albums and songs but were soon going to disband. However, BTS won their first every music show win with their song "I Need U" on May 5, 2015. BTS then won a year later their first major award on MAMA music awards for Artist of the Year on October 21, 2016 which suprised BTS themselves. However, it was the start to their breakful journey.

BTS on the following year attended the AMAs on November 19 2017.

They did an arena and stadium tour gloabl starting with South Korea August 25 2018 with 1,930,642 attendance and ending with $246,512,484.

They did an extension of their 2018 tour of Love Yourself with Speak Yourself ending with 1,930,642 tickets and $246.5 million.

In February 21, the album Map of the Soul was the awaited comeback for BTS and it broke many records. It is the first Korean album in history to sold 5 million copies. It has the highest number of first day sales in South Korea. It earned the #1 title for best selling album from Guinness World Records. It also earned biggest Spotify debut in 2020.

Today, BTS are now in the new building HYBE and have their own stocks of the company. They even are independent from other music of the company as a whole. BTS recieved Grammy nominations as the first Korean acts to do so since 2021. BTS have broken records as a group such as most streamed songs and highest grossing tour renevue. BTS continues to break barriers and give other KPOP acts opportunities with their english singles Butter and Dynamite. Many idols and the president of South Korea praised BTS for paving the way for recognition.
